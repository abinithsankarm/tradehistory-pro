Trading History Timeline

Learn how global trading evolved over time

Ancient Trade (3000 BC)

Barter systems allowed people to exchange goods and services without using money.

Silk Road (130 BC)

The Silk Road connected Asia, Europe, and Africa, creating one of history's largest trade networks.

Medieval Trade Routes

Merchants transported spices, silk, gold, and other valuable goods across continents.

Stock Market (1602)

The Amsterdam Stock Exchange became the world's first modern stock exchange.

Forex Market

International currency trading expanded with modern banking and global commerce.

Electronic Trading (1971)

Computers transformed financial markets through faster and more efficient trading systems.

Cryptocurrency Era (2009)

Bitcoin introduced decentralized digital currency and blockchain technology.
